


-------------- Example Usage 3 --------------

ORIGINAL STEPS:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
2. Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan
3. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and egg in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper
4. Transfer into prepared loaf pan
5. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top
6. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour

MODIFIED STEPS:
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
2. Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan
3. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and egg in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper
4. Transfer into prepared loaf pan
5. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top
6. Steam in the preheated steamer until no longer pink in the center, about 0 minutes
